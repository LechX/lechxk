Lived Locations

I studied abroad in Freiburg and have a strong feeling that I'll return to Germany at some point. I lived in San Francisco, after it was cool. Formal Education

I was Montessori-educated through 8th grade. Still a big believer in prepared environments and carrying chairs the proper way. I was in the first graduating class at my high school (go Knights!) which prepared me for life at a startup. I went to Pomona College, happiest place on earth. I'm currently enrolled at PDX Code Guild, honing my Python and various other computer languages.

Major Employers

I worked for a metals recycler, Schnitzer, in a variety of capacities -- strategy, analytics, design, project management, and directing philanthropic efforts. I worked at Bain, consulting on revenue-generation, cost-reduction, and private equity diligence projects in a variety of industries, including healthcare, consumer products, utilities, retail, and technology. I helped build Shift, an online automotive marketplace, from 10 to 300 employees. My primary responsibility was developing valuation products and processes (appraisal and retail), but wore as many hats as necessary to drive growth.
